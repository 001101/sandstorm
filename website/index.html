Sandstorm

Personal Cloud Sandbox

Sandstorm is an upcoming technology which puts you, the user, in control of your cloud apps. Sandstorm gives you a personal server in the cloud where your apps run, at a small fraction of the price of a typical cloud VM. Apps can be installed through an app-store-like interface and are accessed through your browser just like the web apps you use today. However, the app server runs in a secure sandbox that works for you. The app's developer cannot access your personal data and cannot take the app away from you. You retain complete control.

If you are a developer, the benefit of developing for Sandstorm is simple: zero costs. You do not have to pay for or maintain servers. You do not have to worry about how to scale. You do not have to carry a pager. You do not have to implement authentication or sharing -- every app gets these automatically. You just make your app work, throw it on the store, and you're done. Your apps can be written on top of any technology stack that runs on Linux -- no need to learn a new language. Apps can trivially expose APIs and integrate with each other using Cap'n Proto -- no need for complex OAuth handshakes. Easy integration means that even a simple app can participate and create value in the Sandstorm ecosystem.

Sandstorm is open source software. You will always be able to run your own instance on your own hardware.
